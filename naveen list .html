doughnuts preparation

There are two kinds of doughnuts: cake and yeast. Cake doughnuts (my personal favorite) are made with a sweet dough leavened by baking powder. These doughnuts have a tender, spongy interior with a slightly crispy exterior. Yeast doughnuts, on the other hand, use yeast to help the dough rise, becoming fluffy and light in the process.
